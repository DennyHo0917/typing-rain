Custom Lists For Homeschool Lessons

Homeschool spelling work often follows a curriculum, workbook, or parent-made list. Generic spelling games are fun, but they rarely match the exact custom word list being taught that week.

Weekly Routine

  • Paste the current lesson's spelling words.
  • Run a short spelling test with your own words.
  • Replay missed words until the list feels comfortable.
  • Save or share the no-login link for another practice session.

Small Enough To Reuse Every Week

No lesson builder or platform setup. Paste the exact words, play the round, and use missed-word replay before the weekly spelling check.

Quick FAQ

Can I use homeschool curriculum words? Yes. Paste words from a curriculum, workbook, or parent-made list.

Can I run a spelling test with my own words? Yes. Start a short round with the exact words you entered.

Does it require a homeschool platform account? No. It works in the browser with a shareable no-login link.
