Why Homeschool Families Need Custom Lists

Homeschool spelling work often follows a curriculum, workbook, or parent-made list. Generic spelling games are fun, but they rarely match the exact words being taught that week.

Weekly Routine

  • Paste the current lesson's spelling words.
  • Let the student play one short round.
  • Replay missed words until the list feels comfortable.
  • Save or share the link for another practice session.
