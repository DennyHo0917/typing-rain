What Problem This Solves

Parents, teachers, tutors, and homeschool families often already have the word list. The hard part is getting a student to practice it more than once without building a lesson, creating an account, or searching through fixed spelling games that use unrelated words.

How My Spelling Game Works

  • Paste the spelling words from homework, a quiz sheet, or a curriculum list.
  • Play a falling-word typing round using only those words.
  • Replay missed words so practice focuses on what still needs work.
  • Copy a practice link and share the same list with no login.
