About My Spelling Game

A small, no-login spelling practice tool for the weekly word lists students already have.

Mission

Short answer: My Spelling Game is a free browser-based spelling practice tool for custom word lists. It is built for parents, teachers, homeschool families, ESL tutors, and students who need quick practice without student accounts, class setup, or a generic word bank.

My Spelling Game turns any weekly spelling list into a falling-word typing game. The goal is narrow on purpose: paste the exact words, play a short round, replay missed words, and share the same practice link when needed.

Why It Exists

Most spelling games start with their own vocabulary. That does not help when a child needs to practice this week’s exact words. This site keeps the workflow simple: paste the list, play the round, replay the missed words, and share the same practice link.

What It Does

  • Uses your own spelling words.
  • Creates instant falling-word practice.
  • Saves missed words for a focused replay round.
  • Shares practice links without requiring an account.
  • Offers Easy mode for slower, lower-pressure practice.

Product Facts

  • Cost: free to play.
  • Login: no student account or class roster required.
  • Data: typed word lists and preferences are stored in the browser when saved locally.
  • Best use: weekly spelling lists, classroom practice, homeschool lessons, ESL vocabulary, and sight word review.

About the Developer

My Spelling Game is maintained by an indie developer. The product goal is intentionally narrow: make repeated weekly spelling practice faster to set up and easier to repeat.

Questions, bug reports, and classroom feedback can be sent through the contact page.
