Turn Any Spelling List Into a Typing Game

SpellRain is a custom spelling words game for parents, teachers, homeschool families, and ESL tutors who already have a weekly word list and need a quick way to make practice feel less repetitive.

Why Custom Spelling Words Matter

Most spelling games use a fixed word bank. That is fine for generic typing practice, but it does not help when a student needs to practice this week's exact words. SpellRain lets you paste your own list, play immediately, and keep the practice focused on the words that will appear on the next quiz.

  • Use your own spelling list: Paste words from a teacher handout, homework sheet, or homeschool curriculum.
  • Replay missed words: Words that fall to the bottom are saved for another round.
  • Share a practice link: Copy a link that includes the same word list, with no account or class setup.
  • Optional read-aloud: Use your browser's built-in speech voice for extra listening practice.

Good For Weekly Spelling Practice

This tool is intentionally small. It is for the repeated weekly job: take a short spelling list, make a child practice it, find the missed words, then practice those again. No student accounts, no lesson builder, no platform lock-in.
