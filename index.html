Free Spelling Game And Test With Your Own Words

My Spelling Game is a free custom spelling words game for parents, teachers, homeschool families, classroom centers, and ESL tutors who already have a weekly word list and need a quick way to make practice feel less repetitive.

Why A Custom Spelling List Matters

Most spelling games use a fixed word bank. That is fine for generic typing practice, but it does not help when a student needs to practice this week's exact words. My Spelling Game lets you paste your own custom word list, play immediately, and keep the practice focused on the words that will appear on the next quiz or spelling test.

  • Use your own custom word list: Paste words from a teacher handout, homework sheet, grade-level list, or homeschool curriculum.
  • Run a quick spelling test: Students type the falling words from the exact list you entered.
  • Use missed-word replay: Words that fall to the bottom are saved for another round.
  • Share a practice link: Copy a link that includes the same word list, with no account, student login, or class setup.
  • Hear each word: Use your browser's built-in speech voice for optional dictation-style practice.

Built For Teachers, Parents, Homeschool, And ESL

This tool is intentionally small. It is for the repeated weekly job: take a short spelling list, make a child practice it, find the missed words, then practice those again. It is free to play, with no student accounts, no lesson builder, and no platform lock-in.

Practice Pages By Use Case

Use the custom spelling words game when you want a paste-and-play page, the spelling list game for a teacher handout, the weekly spelling practice page for quiz week, the homeschool spelling practice page for curriculum lists, the sight word typing game for early readers, or the vocabulary typing game for ESL and tutoring.

Quick FAQ

Can I use my own spelling words? Yes. Paste a weekly spelling list and the game will use those exact words.

Can I use this as a spelling test with my own words? Yes. Paste a custom word list, start a round, and use the results plus missed-word replay for a quick spelling test.

Can teachers share a custom spelling list with a class? Yes. Copy the practice link so students can open the same list without an account.

Can students replay missed words? Yes. Missed words are saved at the end of the round for focused replay.

Do students need an account? No. My Spelling Game works in the browser with no student account, class setup, or login.
